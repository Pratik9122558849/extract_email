Privacy Policy

Cnetinfosystem - Email Extractor is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension operates entirely on your local device and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data or browsing information with external servers or third parties.

What information does the extension access?

The extension scans the visible text content of web pages you visit to extract email addresses. These email addresses are stored locally on your device using Chrome’s storage API. No other data from your browsing activity is accessed or collected.

How is your data used?

Extracted email addresses are saved only for your personal use within the extension. You can view, copy, or clear this data at any time through the extension’s popup interface. The extension does not send any data outside your device.

Third-party sharing

No information is shared with third parties. All data remains private and local to your browser.

Permissions

The extension requests permissions such as access to tabs, scripting, storage, and notifications solely to provide its core functionality of extracting and managing email addresses. Host permissions are required to allow extraction from any website you visit.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the extension, please contact us at info@cnetinfosystem.com.
